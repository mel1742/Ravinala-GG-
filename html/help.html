Welcome to the help section of RAVIN'ALA Sôma gasy

This game consists in finding the appropriate pair for each card, there are twelve (12) pairs in total. Each of them represents a picture of typical leaves which exists in Madagascar, that's why the game is entitled Ravin'ala. "Ravina" means leaf in Malagasy langage. Enjoy the party ^_^
